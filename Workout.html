Workout Split

Everything is 4 sets by 10 reps

  • Monday: Chest Day 1 (Upper Focus + Abs)
  • Tuesday: Legs
  • Wednesday: Shoulders
  • Thursday: Chest Day 2 (Upper Focus)
  • Friday: Biceps + Abs
  • Saturday: Back
  • Sunday: Rest or Light Active Recovery

Workout Plan

Monday – Chest (Upper Focus) + Abs
  • Incline Dumbbell Flies
  • Incline Dumbbell Bench Press
  • Incline Machine Flies
  • Incline Machine Bench Press
  • Incline Cable Flies

Abs Superset (3 rounds):

  • Side Crunches 4 x 20 each side
  • Dumbbell Side Tilts 4 x 15 each side
  • Machine Ab Crunch 4 x 10
Tuesday – Legs
  • Sissy Squat with Dumbbell
  • Sumo Kettlebell Swing
  • Sumo Squat (can use dumbbell or barbell)
  • Single Leg Extension Machine (each leg)
  • Single Leg Curl Machine (each leg, isolated)
Wednesday – Shoulders
  • Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raise
  • Front Raise (Dumbbell or Plate)
  • Cable Lateral Raise
  • Seated Shoulder Press Machine
Thursday – Chest (Upper Focus, same as Monday)
  • Incline Dumbbell Flies
  • Incline Dumbbell Bench Press
  • Incline Machine Flies
  • Incline Machine Bench Press
  • Incline Cable Flies

(Optional Abs Finisher – same as Monday)

Friday – Biceps + Abs
  • Dumbbell Curl
  • Hammer Curl
  • Front Cable Curl
  • Behind-the-Back Cable Curl

Abs Superset (3 rounds):

  • Side Crunches x 20 each side
  • Regular Crunches x 30
  • Dumbbell Side Tilts x 15 each side
Saturday – Back
  • Wide-Grip Lat Pulldown to Chest
  • Pull-Ups (bodyweight or assisted)
  • Seated Single Arm Cable Row
  • Dumbbell Row (one arm or both)
Sunday – Rest

Do something that feels good.
